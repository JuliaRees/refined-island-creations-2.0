Our Process

1.

Schedule a call with us!

During this consultation, we'll explore your needs and goals, creating a scope of work and laying the groundwork for your customized website. This is crucial so that your website truly reflects the essence of your luxury property. Let's start building your digital sanctuary today.

Book A Call Today!

2.

Wireframe & Moodboard

Next, we'll create a sitemap, wireframe, and mood board based on your vision. This blueprint will give you a preview of the design's look and feel, aligning with your brand identity and goals.

3.

Website Design - Bringing Your Vision to Life

Our team will design a customized website tailored to your brand and goals. Using placeholder content and images, you'll see the captivating essence of your website come alive.

4.

Coding Your Website - Making It Real

Once the design is approved, we will build your website using HTML, CSS, jQuery & Jekyll for a seamless digital experience. At this stage, you'll have the opportunity to review your website on a temporary domain for feedback.

5.

Testing & Connect Live Domain - Your Website Goes Live

Before the big reveal, we'll test your website's performance on different devices and browsers to ensure fast loading and impressive appearance. After making final adjustments, we'll connect your domain, and your customized website will be ready for the world to see.

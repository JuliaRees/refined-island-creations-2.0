Our Process

1.

Schedule a call with us!

During this consultation, we'll dive deep into understanding your needs and goals. We'll outline an approximate scope of work, laying the groundwork for your bespoke website. Your vision and aspirations form the core of this crucial step, ensuring a tailored solution that reflects your luxury property's unique essence. Let's begin crafting your digital sanctuary today.

Book A Call Today!

2.

Wireframe & Moodboard

Next, we'll craft a sitemap, wireframe, and mood board inspired by your vision. This visual blueprint will provide a preview of the design's look and feel, ensuring that we align with your brand identity and aspirations.

3.

Website Design - Bringing Your Vision to Life

Our expert team will work diligently to design your website tailored to suit your brand and goals. With placeholder content and images, you'll witness the website's captivating essence come to life.

4.

Coding Your Website - Making It Real

Once the design is approved, we'll begin building your website using HTML, CSS, jQuery & Jekyll, crafting a seamless digital experience. At this stage, you'll have the opportunity to review your website on a temporary domain for feedback.

5.

Testing & Connect Live Domain - Your Website Goes Live

Before the grand reveal, we'll conduct performance tests, ensuring your website loads quickly and looks impressive across devices and browsers. Once the final touches are in place, we'll connect your domain, and your bespoke website will be ready for the world to experience.
